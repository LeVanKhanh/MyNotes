# Factors for Satisfaction
Factors for satisfaction are called motivators.

- Achievement
- Recognition
- The work itself
- Responsibility
- Advancement
- Growth

# Factors for Dissatisfaction
Factors for dissatisfaction are called hygiene factors.

- Company policies
- Supervision
- Relationship with supervisor and peers
- Work conditions
- Salary
- Status
- Security
